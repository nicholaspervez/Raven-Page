So why ravens, eh?

Ravens, a subcategory of corvids, are among the most intelligent of animals on our planet. This combined with their haunting appearance reinforced by folklore going centuries back makes for a really interesting being and one we should all learn more about!

Did you know?

  • Ravens remember human faces, so don't piss them off!
  • Ravens will hide food from other ravens, but beware - sometimes they will pretend to hide food in a place as a decoy and then actually hide the food somewhere else!
  • They can mimic human voices more convincingly than parrots!
  • They are the only animals other than primates to have been observed communicating nonvocally with eachother, for example pointing with thier beaks!
